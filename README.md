## <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/YOUR%20AI%20COACH-3E63DD?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=181818" height="26"/> A coach that programs for you

No more copying someone else's spreadsheet. BoulderTrain designs a structured, multi-week training block tailored to *your* level and *your* schedule &mdash; then spaces the hard sessions, keeps variety high, and never stacks two finger-frying days back to back. You just show up and climb.

<br/>

## <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/BUILT%20TO%20PEAK-3E63DD?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=181818" height="26"/> Real periodization, not random workouts

Your plan is built in cycles &mdash; progressive build weeks that ramp the load, followed by lighter **deload** weeks so you recover and come back stronger. You can see exactly where you are in the block and what's coming next, so every session has a purpose.

<br/>

## <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/ADAPTS%20TO%20YOU-3E63DD?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=181818" height="26"/> It listens to your body

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <b>Reads your fatigue</b><br/>
      Logging tough, drained sessions? It eases the next ones off so you don't dig a hole.
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <b>Protects your fingers</b><br/>
      Flag a niggle and it backs off max-intensity finger work until you're ready.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <b>Fits your real week</b><br/>
      Always skip Mondays? It stops scheduling the sessions you never do.
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <b>Grows with you</b><br/>
      Send a new grade and your plan raises the ceiling to keep you progressing.
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

<br/>

## <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/A%20SESSION%20FOR%20EVERY%20DAY-3E63DD?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=181818" height="26"/> Hundreds of climbing workouts

**390+ dedicated bouldering workouts** covering everything from **V2 warm-ups to V15 projects** &mdash; power, power-endurance, finger strength, core, conditioning, movement and mobility. Whatever gear you've got (wall, hangboard, campus board, weights, or just bodyweight), there's a session that fits.

<br/>

## <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/YOURS%2C%20EVERYWHERE-3E63DD?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=181818" height="26"/> Private and offline
